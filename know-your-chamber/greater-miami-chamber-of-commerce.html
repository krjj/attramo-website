About Greater Miami Chamber of Commerce

Greater Miami Chamber of Commerce is a leading membership-driven local chamber located in Miami, Florida. It has members all across Miami metropolitan area, South Florida, Fort Lauderdale, West Palm Beach. It was founded in 1907 and is a registered member of the U.S. Chamber of Commerce.

Greater Miami Chamber of Commerce’s mission statement broadly covers and conveys - The organisation is a catalyst to help members grow, improve and protect their businesses. It is dedicated to building the best environment for business in the Americas and committed to improving the economic vitality of South Florida by educating and advocating for solutions to members’ business needs.

Working as a Miami’s small business community, the Chamber has created strategic partnerships within the local business network, which has resulted in a mutually beneficial relationship.

Greater Miami Chamber of Commerce is committed to focus on Government Affairs, Industry Growth, International Business Growth. It leverages the expertise of local government, city council members and community representatives to develop pro-business initiatives. For strengthening, referring and connecting local businesses, the organization also works with Small Business Development Centers (SBDCs).

The organization helps small businesses in getting name in online membership directory, access to exclusive resources, valuable partnership opportunities through business - event tie ups, SEO benefits with website citation, exclusive mailing list access, relocation and expansion support, providing market information, engaging with like-minded peers, member directory listing, providing group health, opportunity to be listed in the chamber of commerce visitor guide, promoting broader pro-business initiatives, new member mentoring program, building and establishing business credibility, improving your business visibility, providing legislative services, directing access to chamber events and programs, free publicity with local business promotional guides, members-only discounts and services.

The Chamber has many benefits, services and discounts that it offers to its membership holders. Some of the benefits include relocation aid, supporting women entrepreneurship, budgeting and financial forecasting, improving operational efficiency, understanding international trade commission, getting affordable small business insurance, finding federal government contracting opportunities, managing business finances, buying and leasing business equipment’s, business relocating or expanding to new locations help, import and export assistance, technical assistance training, getting help in state and federal online business registration, developing HR policies and human resource management, setting up business expansion strategies, buying a business or franchise, small business loans and commercial finance, building a successful social entrepreneurship and many more.

The trade union has a wide variety of events from monthly networking mixers to annual luncheons to build and enhance member businesses. It offers engaging events such as HYPE Miami Awards, Trustee Luncheon, The Business of Education, Health Care Heroes Awards Luncheon, Annual Meeting.

Greater Miami Chamber of Commerce has partnered with Bank of America, Wells Fargo, Baptist Health South Florida, CareerSource South Florida, Florida International University in Miami, Carnival Corporation for the economic development and growth of greater Miami, Florida.
