About JAX Chamber of Commerce

JAX Chamber of Commerce is a leading membership-driven local chamber located in Jacksonville, Florida. It has members all across Jacksonville metropolitan area, St. Marys, Palatka. It is a registered member of U.S. Chamber of Commerce.

JAX Chamber of Commerce’s mission statement broadly covers and conveys - To drive economic growth in Northeast Florida

Working as a Jacksonville’s small business community, the Chamber has created strategic partnerships within the local business network, which has resulted in a mutually beneficial relationship.

JAX Chamber of Commerce is committed to focus on Local Business, Economic Development, Women in Business. It leverages the expertise of local government, city council members and community representatives to develop pro-business initiatives. For strengthening, referring and connecting local businesses, the organization also works with Small Business Development Centers (SBDCs).

The organization helps small businesses in member directory listing, providing group health, opportunity to be listed in the chamber of commerce visitor guide, promoting broader pro-business initiatives, new member mentoring program, developing business network, providing legislative services, directing access to chamber events and programs, free publicity with local business promotional guides, members-only discounts and services, getting name in online membership directory, access to exclusive resources, valuable partnership opportunities through business - event tie ups, SEO benefits with website citation, influencing government by becoming a voice of business, free chamber newsletter with offers and other local businesses information, getting more customer referrals, exclusive mailing list access, marketing and sponsorship opportunities through digital as well as physical mailers, added promotion through chambers social media.

The Chamber has many benefits, services and discounts that it offers to its membership holders. Some of the benefits include recruitment and finding right candidate, understanding international trade commission, getting affordable small business insurance, improving operational efficiency, improving marketing strategies, finding federal government contracting opportunities, managing business finances, buying and leasing business equipment’s, business relocating or expanding to new locations help, applying and getting permit and vendor license, developing HR policies and human resource management, small business administration, creating a business plan and building entrepreneurial skills, setting up business expansion strategies, supporting women entrepreneurship, budgeting and financial forecasting and many more.

The trade union has a wide variety of events from monthly networking mixers to annual luncheons to build and enhance member businesses. It offers engaging events such as South Council Luncheon, ImpactJAX 101, JAX Chamber Trade Show, Downtown Council Breakfast, Professional Women's Council Luncheon, Small Business Center Orientation & Breakout Sessions, Transportation & Logistics Council Luncheon, Jacksonville IT Council Breakfast.

JAX Chamber of Commerce has partnered with BDO Usa, Wells Fargo, VyStar Credit Union, SunTrust Banks, JP Morgan Chase Bank for the economic development and growth of greater Jacksonville, Florida.
