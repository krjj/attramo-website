About Greater Omaha Chamber of Commerce

Greater Omaha Chamber of Commerce is a leading membership-driven local chamber located in Omaha, Nebraska. It has members all across Downtown Omaha, East Omaha. It is a registered member of U.S. Chamber of Commerce.

Greater Omaha Chamber of Commerce’s mission statement broadly covers and conveys - To champion a thriving business community and a prosperous region through visionary leadership and collaboration.

Working as a Omaha’s small business community, the Chamber has created strategic partnerships within the local business network, which has resulted in a mutually beneficial relationship.

Greater Omaha Chamber of Commerce is committed to focus on Economic Development, Business Advocacy, Startups. It leverages the expertise of local government, city council members and community representatives to develop pro-business initiatives. For strengthening, referring and connecting local businesses, the organization also works with Small Business Development Centers (SBDCs).

The organization helps small businesses in engaging with like-minded peers, SEO benefits with website citation, exclusive mailing list access, portraying yourself as a local expert in your business type, marketing and sponsorship opportunities through digital as well as physical mailers, providing market information, member directory listing, providing group health, opportunity to be listed in the chamber of commerce visitor guide, building and establishing business credibility, improving your business visibility, free chamber newsletter with offers and other local businesses information, getting more customer referrals, business owners can attend ongoing training & education sessions, business advocacy for your business, getting name in online membership directory, access to exclusive resources.

The Chamber has many benefits, services and discounts that it offers to its membership holders. Some of the benefits include technical assistance training, understanding international trade commission, getting affordable small business insurance, finding federal government contracting opportunities, managing business finances, buying and leasing business equipment’s, business relocating or expanding to new locations help, import and export assistance, starting and growing new business, getting help in state and federal online business registration, small business administration, creating a business plan and building entrepreneurial skills, small business loans and commercial finance, building a successful social entrepreneurship, relocation aid, supporting women entrepreneurship, budgeting and financial forecasting and many more.

The trade union has a wide variety of events from monthly networking mixers to annual luncheons to build and enhance member businesses. It offers engaging events such as Creative Mornings, Small Business Day, Member Success orientation, Mindset Leadership Series, Business Excellence Award, Omaha Business Hall of Fame Gala, Economic Outlook, Member Appreciation Holiday.

Greater Omaha Chamber of Commerce has partnered with many reputed companies and organisation for the economic development and growth of greater Omaha, Nebraska.
