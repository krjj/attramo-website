About Fort Worth Chamber of Commerce

Fort Worth Chamber of Commerce is a leading membership-driven local chamber located in Fort Worth, Texas. It has members all across Fort Worth City. It is a registered member of U.S. Chamber of Commerce.

Fort Worth Chamber of Commerce’s mission statement broadly covers and conveys - To bring the Fort Worth region together to identify issues, solve problems and help align resources, resulting in a stronger business climate and greater economic prosperity for all.

Working as a Fort Worth’s small business community, the Chamber has created strategic partnerships within the local business network, which has resulted in a mutually beneficial relationship.

Fort Worth Chamber of Commerce is committed to focus on Small Business, Economic Development. It leverages the expertise of local government, city council members and community representatives to develop pro-business initiatives. For strengthening, referring and connecting local businesses, the organization also works with Small Business Development Centers (SBDCs).

The organization helps small businesses in developing business network, influencing government by becoming a voice of business, free chamber newsletter with offers and other local businesses information, providing legislative services, directing access to chamber events and programs, free publicity with local business promotional guides, members-only discounts and services, business advocacy for your business, getting name in online membership directory, access to exclusive resources, SEO benefits with website citation, exclusive mailing list access, portraying yourself as a local expert in your business type, marketing and sponsorship opportunities through digital as well as physical mailers, providing market information, engaging with like-minded peers, member directory listing, providing group health, opportunity to be listed in the chamber of commerce visitor guide, promoting broader pro-business initiatives, new member mentoring program.

The Chamber has many benefits, services and discounts that it offers to its membership holders. Some of the benefits include starting and growing new business, getting help in state and federal online business registration, developing HR policies and human resource management, creating a business plan and building entrepreneurial skills, setting up business expansion strategies, buying a business or franchise, relocation aid, supporting women entrepreneurship, budgeting and financial forecasting, improving operational efficiency, improving marketing strategies, recruitment and finding right candidate, understanding international trade commission, getting affordable small business insurance, finding federal government contracting opportunities, managing business finances, business relocating or expanding to new locations help, import and export assistance, technical assistance training and many more.

The trade union has a wide variety of events from monthly networking mixers to annual luncheons to build and enhance member businesses. It offers engaging events such as Member Mingle, Ribbon Cutting Events, West Area Council City Update, Impact Your Business Luncheon, The Ladder Alliance Lunch and Learn.

Fort Worth Chamber of Commerce has partnered with PlainsCapital Bank, Southside Bank, Airbus, BBVA Compass Bank, Frost Bank for the economic development and growth of greater Fort Worth, Texas.
