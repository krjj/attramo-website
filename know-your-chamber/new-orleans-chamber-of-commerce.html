About New Orleans Chamber of Commerce

New Orleans Chamber of Commerce is a leading membership-driven local chamber located in New Orleans, Louisiana. It has members all across Greater New Orleans Region. It a registered member of U.S. Chamber of Commerce.

New Orleans Chamber of Commerce’s mission statement broadly covers and conveys - To provide members with the opportunity to build mutually beneficial partnerships within the City of New Orleans.

Working as a New Orleans’s small business community, the Chamber has created strategic partnerships within the local business network, which has resulted in a mutually beneficial relationship.

New Orleans Chamber of Commerce is committed to focus on Business Development and Advocacy. It leverages the expertise of local government, city council members and community representatives to develop pro-business initiatives. For strengthening, referring and connecting local businesses, the organization also works with Small Business Development Centers (SBDCs).

The organization helps small businesses in providing legislative services, portraying yourself as a local expert in your business type, marketing and sponsorship opportunities through digital as well as physical mailers, added promotion through chambers social media, members-only discounts and services, business owners can attend ongoing training and education sessions, business advocacy for your business, getting name in online membership directory, access to exclusive resources, valuable partnership opportunities through business - event tie ups, SEO benefits with website citation, exclusive mailing list access, building and establishing business credibility, improving your business visibility, developing business network, influencing government by becoming a voice of business, free chamber newsletter with offers and other local businesses information, getting more customer referrals, member directory listing, providing group health, opportunity to be listed in the chamber of commerce visitor guide, promoting broader pro-business initiatives, new member mentoring programrelocation and expansion support, providing market information, engaging with like-minded peers.

The Chamber has many benefits, services and discounts that it offers to its membership holders. Some of the benefits include setting up business expansion strategies, developing HR policies and human resource management recruitment and finding right candidate, getting affordable small business insurance, finding federal government contracting opportunities, managing business finances, buying and leasing business equipment’s, business relocating or expanding to new locations help, buying a business or franchise, small business loans and commercial finance, building a successful social entrepreneurship, relocation aid, supporting women entrepreneurship, budgeting and financial forecasting, improving operational efficiency, improving marketing strategies, starting and growing new business, getting help in state and federal online business registration, applying and getting permit and vendor license and many more.

The trade union has a wide variety of events from monthly networking mixers to annual luncheons to build and enhance member businesses. It offers engaging events such as Goldman Sachs 10,000 small businesses program, Business Networking Event, Ribbon Cutting Events, Creative Entrepreneurs Meetup, An Evening with Nicole Lynn Fox, BNI New Orleans Area Interest Meeting, Women’s Business Alliance, Small Business Seminars, Member Orientation, Quarterly business luncheon series.

New Orleans Chamber of Commerce has partnered with many reputed companies and organisations for the economic development and growth of greater New Orleans, Louisiana.
