About Greater Phoenix Chamber of Commerce

Greater Phoenix Chamber of Commerce is a leading membership-driven local chamber located in Phoenix, Arizona. It has members all across Phoenix Metropolitan Area, Salt River Valley. It was founded in 1888 and is a registered member of the U.S. Chamber of Commerce.

Greater Phoenix Chamber of Commerce’s mission statement broadly covers and conveys - To promote regional prosperity, serving as a catalyst for economic vitality and strong communities.

Working as a Phoenix’s small business community, the Chamber has created strategic partnerships within the local business network, which has resulted in a mutually beneficial relationship.

Greater Phoenix Chamber of Commerce is committed to focus on Economic Development, Women in Business. It leverages the expertise of local government, city council members and community representatives to develop pro-business initiatives. For strengthening, referring and connecting local businesses, the organization also works with Small Business Development Centers (SBDCs).

The organization helps small businesses in providing legislative services, SEO benefits with website citation, exclusive mailing list access, portraying yourself as a local expert in your business type, directing access to chamber events and programs, f members-only discounts and services, building and establishing business credibility, improving your business visibility, developing business network, relocation and expansion support, providing market information, engaging with like-minded peers, member directory listing, providing group health, opportunity to be listed in the chamber of commerce visitor guide, influencing government by becoming a voice of business, free chamber newsletter with offers and other local businesses information, getting more customer referrals, business owners can attend ongoing training & education sessions, getting name in online membership directory, access to exclusive resources, valuable partnership opportunities through business - event tie ups.

The Chamber has many benefits, services and discounts that it offers to its membership holders. Some of the benefits include finding federal government contracting opportunities, managing business finances, buying and leasing business equipment’s, business relocating or expanding to new locations help, technical assistance training, setting up business expansion strategies, buying a business or franchise, small business loans and commercial finance, building a successful social entrepreneurship, relocation aid, supporting women entrepreneurship, budgeting and financial forecasting, getting help in state and federal online business registration, applying and getting permit and vendor license, developing HR policies and human resource management, improving marketing strategies, recruitment and finding right candidate, understanding international trade commission, getting affordable small business insurance and many more.

The trade union has a wide variety of events from monthly networking mixers to annual luncheons to build and enhance member businesses. It offers engaging events such as Professional Women’s Alliance Luncheon (PWA), Valley Young Professionals (VYP), After 5 Mixers, Business Showcase, Phoenix Chamber 101, Powerconnect Lunch, Small Business Leadership Forum, Leads Clubs, Economic Development Forum.

Greater Phoenix Chamber of Commerce has partnered with Arizona Public Service (APS), Blue Cross Blue Shield of Arizona, Cox Communications, Salt River Project (SRP), National Bank of Arizona for the economic development and growth of greater Phoenix, Arizona.
