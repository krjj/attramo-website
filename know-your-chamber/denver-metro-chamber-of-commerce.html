About Denver Metro Chamber of Commerce

Denver Metro Chamber of Commerce is a leading membership-driven local chamber located in Denver, Colorado. It has members all across City of Chicago. It was founded in 1867 and is a registered member of the U.S. Chamber of Commerce.

Denver Metro Chamber of Commerce’s mission statement broadly covers and conveys - Everything we do at the Denver Metro Chamber of Commerce is about one thing: Putting more Coloradans to work.

Working as a Denver’s small business community, the Chamber has created strategic partnerships within the local business network, which has resulted in a mutually beneficial relationship.

Denver Metro Chamber of Commerce is committed to focus on Building an Educated Workforce, Powering the Country and Our Economy, Healthy Workforce. It leverages the expertise of local government, city council members and community representatives to develop pro-business initiatives. For strengthening, referring and connecting local businesses, the organization also works with Small Business Development Centers (SBDCs).

The organization helps small businesses in building and establishing business credibility, improving your business visibility, developing business network, influencing government by becoming a voice of business, business owners can attend ongoing training and education sessions, business advocacy for your business, engaging with like-minded peers, member directory listing, providing group health, opportunity to be listed in the chamber of commerce visitor guide, promoting broader pro-business initiatives, new member mentoring program, exclusive mailing list access, portraying yourself as a local expert in your business type, marketing and sponsorship opportunities through digital as well as physical mailers, added promotion through chambers social media, relocation and expansion support, providing market information.

The Chamber has many benefits, services and discounts that it offers to its membership holders. Some of the benefits include getting help in state and federal online business registration, relocation aid, supporting women entrepreneurship, budgeting and financial forecasting, improving operational efficiency, improving marketing strategies, recruitment and finding right candidate, finding federal government contracting opportunities, managing business finances, buying and leasing business equipment’s, business relocating or expanding to new locations help, applying and getting permit and vendor license, small business loans and commercial finance, building a successful social entrepreneurship, import and export assistance, technical assistance training and many more.

The trade union has a wide variety of events from monthly networking mixers to annual luncheons to build and enhance member businesses. It offers engaging events such as Annual Meeting, Boots 'n Business, Business Awards, Challenge Campaign, Colorado Business Hall of Fame, Golf Classic, Metro Denver EDC Annual Meeting, Metro Denver EDC Site Selection Conference, State of Small Business.

Denver Metro Chamber of Commerce has partnered with Centurylink, FirstBank, HealthONE, Wells Fargo, Xcel Energy for the economic development and growth of greater Denver, Colorado.
