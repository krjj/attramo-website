About Dallas Regional Chamber of Commerce

Dallas Regional Chamber of Commerce is a leading membership-driven regional chamber located in Dallas, Texas. It has members all across Dallas-Fort Worth area, North Texas, Central Dallas, East Dallas, Southern Dallas. It is a registered member of U.S. Chamber of Commerce.

Dallas Regional Chamber of Commerce’s mission statement broadly covers and conveys - Together, we will lead the Dallas Region to become the most economically prosperous region - and the most desirable place to live, work, and do business - in the United States.

Working as a Dallas’s small business community, the Chamber has created strategic partnerships within the local business network, which has resulted in a mutually beneficial relationship.

Dallas Regional Chamber of Commerce is committed to focus on Economic Development, Talent Attraction, Public Policy and Advocacy. It leverages the expertise of local government, city council members and community representatives to develop pro-business initiatives. For strengthening, referring and connecting local businesses, the organization also works with Small Business Development Centers (SBDCs).

The organization helps small businesses in building and establishing business credibility, improving your business visibility, developing business network, free chamber newsletter with offers and other local businesses information, getting more customer referrals, providing legislative services, directing access to chamber events and programs, business owners can attend ongoing training and education sessions, valuable partnership opportunities through business - event tie ups, SEO benefits with website citation, marketing and sponsorship opportunities through digital as well as physical mailers, opportunity to be listed in the chamber of commerce visitor guide, promoting broader pro-business initiatives, new member mentoring program.

The Chamber has many benefits, services and discounts that it offers to its membership holders. Some of the benefits include starting and growing new business, getting help in state and federal online business registration, small business administration, creating a business plan and building entrepreneurial skills, setting up business expansion strategies, buying a business or franchise, budgeting and financial forecasting, improving operational efficiency, improving marketing strategies, recruitment and finding right candidate, understanding international trade commission, finding federal government contracting opportunities, managing business finances, import and export assistance and many more.

The trade union has a wide variety of events from monthly networking mixers to annual luncheons to build and enhance member businesses. It offers engaging events such as Women's Business Conference, Member Happy Hour, Annual Meeting.

Dallas Regional Chamber of Commerce has partnered with many reputed companies and organisation for the economic development and growth of greater Dallas, Texas.
