About Greater Brighton Area Chamber of Commerce

Greater Brighton Area Chamber of Commerce is a leading membership-driven local chamber located in Brighton, Michigan. It has members all across Livingston County and southeast Michigan. It is a registered member of U.S. Chamber of Commerce.

Greater Brighton Area Chamber of Commerce’s mission statement broadly covers and conveys - chamber wants to promote opportunities for a prosperous business community.

Working as a Brighton’s small business community, the Chamber has created strategic partnerships within the local business network, which has resulted in a mutually beneficial relationship.

Greater Brighton Area Chamber of Commerce is committed to focus on Economic Impact, Environmental issues, Young Professionals, Empowering Professional Women's. It leverages the expertise of local government, city council members and community representatives to develop pro-business initiatives. For strengthening, referring and connecting local businesses, the organization also works with Small Business Development Centers (SBDCs).

The organization helps small businesses in building and establishing business credibility, improving your business visibility, developing business network, business owners can attend ongoing training and education sessions, business advocacy for your business, getting name in online membership directory, access to exclusive resources, valuable partnership opportunities through business - event tie ups, SEO benefits with website citation, exclusive mailing list access, influencing government by becoming a voice of business, free publicity with local business promotional guides, members-only discounts and services.

The Chamber has many benefits, services and discounts that it offers to its membership holders. Some of the benefits include small business administration, creating a business plan and building entrepreneurial skills, buying a business or franchise, small business loans and commercial finance, relocation aid, supporting women entrepreneurship, budgeting and financial forecasting, improving operational efficiency, recruitment and finding right candidate, understanding international trade commission, managing business finances, buying and leasing business equipment’s, business relocating or expanding to new locations help, starting and growing new business, getting help in state and federal online business registration, applying and getting permit and vendor license, developing HR policies and human resource management and many more.

The trade union has a wide variety of events from monthly networking mixers to annual luncheons to build and enhance member businesses. It offers engaging events such as Brighton B2B, Midday Connect, Livingston Business Alliance, Brighton B2B, Economic Development Power Lunch, Senior Leaders Network of Livingston County, Grand Re-Opening and Ribbon Cutting, Tech Talk.

Greater Brighton Area Chamber of Commerce has partnered with Michigan Manufacturing Technology Center, Michigan Small Business Development Center for the economic development and growth of greater Brighton, Michigan.
