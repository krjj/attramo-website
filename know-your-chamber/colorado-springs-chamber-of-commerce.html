About Colorado Springs Chamber of Commerce

Colorado Springs Chamber of Commerce is a leading membership-driven local chamber located in Colorado Springs, Colorado. It has members all across Colorado Springs, Calhan, Green Mountain Falls, Manitou Springs. It is a registered member of U.S. Chamber of Commerce.

Colorado Springs Chamber of Commerce’s mission statement broadly covers and conveys - To enhance the quality of our community by serving the business development needs of our region so that economic growth exceeds population growth.

Working as a Colorado Springs’s small business community, the Chamber has created strategic partnerships within the local business network, which has resulted in a mutually beneficial relationship.

Colorado Springs Chamber of Commerce is committed to focus on Military Affairs, Aerospace and Defense, Business Development. It leverages the expertise of local government, city council members and community representatives to develop pro-business initiatives. For strengthening, referring and connecting local businesses, the organization also works with Small Business Development Centers (SBDCs).

The organization helps small businesses in influencing government by becoming a voice of business, free chamber newsletter with offers and other local businesses information, providing legislative services, directing access to chamber events and programs, free publicity with local business promotional guides, getting name in online membership directory, valuable partnership opportunities through business - event tie ups, exclusive mailing list access, portraying yourself as a local expert in your business type, added promotion through chambers social media, relocation and expansion support, providing market information, engaging with like-minded peers, member directory listing, promoting broader pro-business initiatives, new member mentoring program.

The Chamber has many benefits, services and discounts that it offers to its membership holders. Some of the benefits include small business administration, creating a business plan and building entrepreneurial skills, buying a business or franchise, building a successful social entrepreneurship, relocation aid, supporting women entrepreneurship, improving operational efficiency, improving marketing strategies, recruitment and finding right candidate, understanding international trade commission, finding federal government contracting opportunities, managing business finances, buying and leasing business equipment’s, business relocating or expanding to new locations help, technical assistance training and many more.

The trade union has a wide variety of events from monthly networking mixers to annual luncheons to build and enhance member businesses. It offers engaging events such as Chamber Connect, Ribbon Cutting, Board of Governors Quarterly Update, Business After Hours, Annual Meetings.

Colorado Springs Chamber of Commerce has partnered with many reputed companies and organisations for the economic development and growth of greater Colorado Springs, Colorado.
