About Los Angeles Chamber of Commerce

Los Angeles Chamber of Commerce is a leading membership-driven local chamber located in Los Angeles, California. It has members all across Los Angeles, Los Angeles County, Orange County, Ventura County, Inland Empire. It was founded in 1888 and is a registered member of the U.S. Chamber of Commerce.

Los Angeles Chamber of Commerce’s mission statement broadly covers and conveys - We champions economic prosperity and quality of life for the Los Angeles region by being the voice of business, promoting collaboration and helping members grow.

Working as a Los Angeles’s small business community, the Chamber has created strategic partnerships within the local business network, which has resulted in a mutually beneficial relationship.

Los Angeles Chamber of Commerce is committed to focus on Business Advocacy, Small Business Success, Global Trade & Foreign Investment, Innovation & Technology, Education Excellence & Talent Development. It leverages the expertise of local government, city council members and community representatives to develop pro-business initiatives. For strengthening, referring and connecting local businesses, the organization also works with Small Business Development Centers (SBDCs).

The Chamber has many benefits, services and discounts that it offers to its membership holders. Some of the benefits include setting up business expansion strategies, buying a business or franchise, small business loans and commercial finance, building a successful social entrepreneurship, relocation aid, supporting women entrepreneurship, budgeting and financial forecasting, improving operational efficiency, improving marketing strategies, recruitment and finding right candidate, understanding international trade commission, starting and growing new business, getting help in state and federal online business registration, applying and getting permit and vendor license, small business administration, creating a business plan and building entrepreneurial skills, getting affordable small business insurance, finding federal government contracting opportunities, managing business finances, buying and leasing business equipment’s, business relocating or expanding to new locations help, import and export assistance, technical assistance training and many more.

The trade union has a wide variety of events from monthly networking mixers to annual luncheons to build and enhance member businesses. It offers engaging events such as Business After Hours Mixers, Referral Network, Small Business Owners Roundtable, Westside Connect.

Los Angeles Chamber of Commerce has partnered with many reputed companies and organisations for the economic development and growth of greater Los Angeles, California.
