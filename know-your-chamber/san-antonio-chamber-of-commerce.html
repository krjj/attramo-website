About San Antonio Chamber of Commerce

San Antonio Chamber of Commerce is a leading membership-driven local chamber located in San Antonio, Texas. It has members all across Downtown San Antonio, Southtown. It was founded in 1894 and is a registered member of the U.S. Chamber of Commerce.

San Antonio Chamber of Commerce’s mission statement broadly covers and conveys - The organization is to be an advocate for all of our members and the business community in building and sustaining a diverse and prosperous economy.

Working as a San Antonio’s small business community, the Chamber has created strategic partnerships within the local business network, which has resulted in a mutually beneficial relationship.

San Antonio Chamber of Commerce is committed to focus on Education And Workforce Development, Economic Development, Military Affairs, Cybersecurity. It leverages the expertise of local government, city council members and community representatives to develop pro-business initiatives. For strengthening, referring and connecting local businesses, the organization also works with Small Business Development Centers (SBDCs).

The organization helps small businesses in influencing government by becoming a voice of business, new member mentoring program, building and establishing business credibility, developing business network, free chamber newsletter with offers and other local businesses information, influencing government by becoming a voice of business, more customer referrals, providing legislative services, directing access to chamber events and programs, free publicity with local business promotional guides, members-only discounts and services, relocation and expansion support, providing market information, engaging with like-minded peers, member directory listing, providing group health, opportunity to be listed in the chamber of commerce visitor guide, promoting broader pro-business initiatives, getting name in online membership directory, access to exclusive resources, valuable partnership opportunities through business - event tie ups, marketing and sponsorship opportunities through digital as well as physical mailers, added promotion through chambers social media.

The Chamber has many benefits, services and discounts that it offers to its membership holders. Some of the benefits include creating a business plan and building entrepreneurial skills, improving operational efficiency, developing HR policies and human resource management, understanding international trade commission, small business loans and commercial finance, getting affordable small business insurance, finding federal government contracting opportunities, managing business finances, buying and leasing business equipment’s, business relocating or expanding to new locations help, import and export assistance, technical assistance training, improving marketing strategies, recruitment and finding right candidate, setting up business expansion strategies, buying a business or franchise, building a successful social entrepreneurship, getting help in state and federal online business registration, applying and getting permit and vendor license and many more.

The trade union has a wide variety of events from monthly networking mixers to annual luncheons to build and enhance member businesses. It offers engaging events such as Ribbon cutting, Annual San Antonio-Austin VisionWalk, Business Support Workshops.

San Antonio Chamber of Commerce has partnered with AT&T, Bank of America, CPS Energy, Frost Bank for the economic development and growth of greater San Antonio, Texas.
