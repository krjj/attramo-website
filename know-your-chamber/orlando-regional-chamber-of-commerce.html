About Orlando Regional Chamber of Commerce

Orlando Regional Chamber of Commerce is a leading membership-driven regional chamber located in Orlando, Florida. It has members all across Greater Orlando. It was founded in 1913 and is a registered member of the U.S. Chamber of Commerce.

Orlando Regional Chamber of Commerce’s mission statement broadly covers and conveys - we will build upon our powerful business programs, benefiting thousands of Members in seven counties - Brevard, Lake, Orange, Osceola, Polk, Seminole and Volusia.

Working as a Orlando’s small business community, the Chamber has created strategic partnerships within the local business network, which has resulted in a mutually beneficial relationship.

Orlando Regional Chamber of Commerce is committed to focus on Economic Partnership, Regional Entrepreneurship. It leverages the expertise of local government, city council members and community representatives to develop pro-business initiatives. For strengthening, referring and connecting local businesses, the organization also works with Small Business Development Centers (SBDCs).

The organization helps small businesses in providing legislative services, directing access to chamber events and programs, providing group health, opportunity to be listed in the chamber of commerce visitor guide, free publicity with local business promotional guides, valuable partnership opportunities through business - event tie ups, SEO benefits with website citation, exclusive mailing list access, portraying yourself as a local expert in your business type, marketing and sponsorship opportunities through digital as well as physical mailers, promoting broader pro-business initiatives, new member mentoring program, added promotion through chambers social media, getting more customer referrals, members-only discounts and services, business owners can attend ongoing training & education sessions, business advocacy for your business, getting name in online membership directory, access to exclusive resources, relocation and expansion support, providing market information, engaging with like-minded peers, member directory listing.

The Chamber has many benefits, services and discounts that it offers to its membership holders. Some of the benefits include setting up business expansion strategies, buying a business or franchise, small business loans and commercial finance, starting and growing new business, getting help in state and federal online business registration, applying and getting permit and vendor license, improving marketing strategies, recruitment and finding right candidate, understanding international trade commission, getting affordable small business insurance, small business administration, creating a business plan and building entrepreneurial skills, building a successful social entrepreneurship, relocation aid, supporting women entrepreneurship, finding federal government contracting opportunities, managing business finances, buying and leasing business equipment’s and many more.

The trade union has a wide variety of events from monthly networking mixers to annual luncheons to build and enhance member businesses. It offers engaging events such as Business Leadership Roundtable, Business Success Strategies meetings, Ribbon Cutting Celebrations, Chamber Connect events and other Signature Events.

Orlando Regional Chamber of Commerce has partnered with Orlando Economic Partnership, Leadership Orlando for the economic development and growth of greater Orlando, Florida.
