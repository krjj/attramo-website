About Nashville Area Chamber of Commerce

Nashville Area Chamber of Commerce is a leading membership-driven local chamber located in Nashville, Tennessee. It has members all across Cheatham county, Davidson county, Dickson county, Maury county, Montgomery county, Robertson county, Rutherford county, Sumner county, Williamson county, Wilson county. It was founded in 1888 and is a registered member of the U.S. Chamber of Commerce.

Nashville Area Chamber of Commerce’s mission statement broadly covers and conveys - To create economic prosperity by facilitating community leadership.

Working as a Nashville’s small business community, the Chamber has created strategic partnerships within the local business network, which has resulted in a mutually beneficial relationship.

Nashville Area Chamber of Commerce is committed to focus on Health Care, Corporate Operations, Advanced Manufacturing, Supply Chain Management, Music And Entertainment. It leverages the expertise of local government, city council members and community representatives to develop pro-business initiatives. For strengthening, referring and connecting local businesses, the organization also works with Small Business Development Centers (SBDCs).

The organization helps small businesses in portraying yourself as a local expert in your business type, marketing and sponsorship opportunities through digital as well as physical mailers, added promotion through chambers social media, directing access to chamber events and programs, free publicity with local business promotional guides, members-only discounts and services, influencing government by becoming a voice of business, free chamber newsletter with offers and other local businesses information, getting more customer referrals, providing legislative services, business advocacy for your business, getting name in online membership directory, access to exclusive resources, valuable partnership opportunities through business - event tie ups, SEO benefits with website citation, exclusive mailing list access, member directory listing, providing group health, opportunity to be listed in the chamber of commerce visitor guide, promoting broader pro-business initiatives, new member mentoring program.

The Chamber has many benefits, services and discounts that it offers to its membership holders. Some of the benefits include improving marketing strategies, recruitment and finding right candidate, understanding international trade commission, getting affordable small business insurance, finding federal government contracting opportunities, managing business finances, buying and leasing business equipment’s, business relocating or expanding to new locations help, applying and getting permit and vendor license, developing HR policies and human resource management, small business administration, creating a business plan and building entrepreneurial skills, setting up business expansion strategies, buying a business or franchise, small business loans and commercial finance, budgeting and financial forecasting, improving operational efficiency import and export assistance and many more.

The trade union has a wide variety of events from monthly networking mixers to annual luncheons to build and enhance member businesses. It offers engaging events such as Academies of Nashville Awards, Area Advisory Council Events, Business After Hours, Business Studio Events, International Business Council Events, Member Orientation, Nashville Emerging Leader Awards (NELAs).

Nashville Area Chamber of Commerce has partnered with Bank of America, Community Health Systems, First Tennessee Bank, HCA Healthcare, Ingram Industries Inc. for the economic development and growth of greater Nashville, Tennessee.
