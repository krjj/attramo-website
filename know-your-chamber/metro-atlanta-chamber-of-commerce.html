About Metro Atlanta Chamber of Commerce

Metro Atlanta Chamber of Commerce is a leading membership-driven local chamber located in Atlanta, Georgia. It has members all across Atlanta City, Fulton County, DeKalb County. It was founded in 1859 and is a registered member of the U.S. Chamber of Commerce.

Metro Atlanta Chamber of Commerce’s mission statement broadly covers and conveys - The organisation is dedicated to positioning Atlanta as a top-tier global competitor while leading our region to a more prosperous and vibrant future. Our focus is advancing economic growth, enhancing our business climate and improving the quality of life for each and every person who calls Atlanta home.

Working as a Atlanta’s small business community, the Chamber has created strategic partnerships within the local business network, which has resulted in a mutually beneficial relationship.

Metro Atlanta Chamber of Commerce is committed to focus on Economic Development, International Development and Trade. It leverages the expertise of local government, city council members and community representatives to develop pro-business initiatives. For strengthening, referring and connecting local businesses, the organization also works with Small Business Development Centers (SBDCs).

The organization helps small businesses in providing legislative services, directing access to chamber events and programs, getting more customer referrals, free publicity with local business promotional guides, members-only discounts and services, business owners can attend ongoing training & education sessions, exclusive mailing list access, portraying yourself as a local expert in your business type, member directory listing, business advocacy for your business, building and establishing business credibility, improving your business visibility, developing business network, influencing government by becoming a voice of business, free chamber newsletter with offers and other local businesses information, getting name in online membership directory, access to exclusive resources, valuable partnership opportunities through business - event tie ups, providing group health, opportunity to be listed in the chamber of commerce visitor guide, SEO benefits with website citation.

The Chamber has many benefits, services and discounts that it offers to its membership holders. Some of the benefits include buying and leasing business equipment’s, business relocating or expanding to new locations help, import and export assistance, applying and getting permit and vendor license, developing HR policies and human resource management, small business administration, technical assistance training, creating a business plan and building entrepreneurial skills, setting up business expansion strategies, buying a business or franchise, starting and growing new business, getting help in state and federal online business registration, finding federal government contracting opportunities, managing business finances, operational efficiency, improving marketing strategies, recruitment and finding right candidate, understanding international trade commission, getting affordable small business insurance and many more.

The trade union has a wide variety of events from monthly networking mixers to annual luncheons to build and enhance member businesses. It offers engaging events such as Supply Chain Leadership Council, Innovation and Entrepreneurship Committee, State of Education in Metro Atlanta, Global Commerce Council, Member Reception, Non-Profit CEO Roundtable, Annual Women's Leadership Forum.

Metro Atlanta Chamber of Commerce has partnered with Delta, GE, KPMG, PNC, Chase, Pwc for the economic development and growth of greater Atlanta, Georgia.
