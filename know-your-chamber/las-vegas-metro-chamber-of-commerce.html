About Las Vegas Metro Chamber of Commerce

Las Vegas Metro Chamber of Commerce is a leading membership-driven local chamber located in Las Vegas, Nevada. It has members all across Las Vegas, Henderson and North Las Vegas. It was founded in 1929 and is a registered member of the U.S. Chamber of Commerce.

Las Vegas Metro Chamber of Commerce’s mission statement broadly covers and conveys - We are the voice of business – your voice – helping to amplify the needs, challenges, and opportunities for our employers and entrepreneurs.

Working as a Las Vegas’s small business community, the Chamber has created strategic partnerships within the local business network, which has resulted in a mutually beneficial relationship.

Las Vegas Metro Chamber of Commerce is committed to focus on Local priorities, Federal priorities, Advocacy & Government affairs. It leverages the expertise of local government, city council members and community representatives to develop pro-business initiatives. For strengthening, referring and connecting local businesses, the organization also works with Small Business Development Centers (SBDCs).

The organization helps small businesses in marketing and sponsorship opportunities through digital as well as physical mailers, access to exclusive resources, valuable partnership opportunities through business - event tie ups, SEO benefits with website citation, added promotion through chambers social media, providing group health, opportunity to be listed in the chamber of commerce visitor guide, promoting broader pro-business initiatives, influencing government by becoming a voice of business, new member mentoring program, building and establishing business credibility, improving your business visibility, developing business network, free chamber newsletter with offers and other local businesses information, getting more customer referrals, business owners can attend ongoing training & education sessions, business advocacy for your business, getting name in online membership directory.

The Chamber has many benefits, services and discounts that it offers to its membership holders. Some of the benefits include finding federal government contracting opportunities, managing business finances, creating a business plan and building entrepreneurial skills, buying and leasing business equipment’s, business relocating or expanding to new locations help, import and export assistance, technical assistance training, getting help in state and federal online business registration, developing HR policies and human resource management, small business administration, improving marketing strategies, recruitment and finding right candidate, understanding international trade commission, getting affordable small business insurance and many more.

The trade union has a wide variety of events from monthly networking mixers to annual luncheons to build and enhance member businesses. It offers engaging events such as Business Excellence Awards, Business Expo Trade Show, Leadership Advance Training, Leadership Las Vegas Program.

Las Vegas Metro Chamber of Commerce has partnered with Customer Service Excellence, Nevada Drug Card, Office Depot, Nevada Broadcasters Association & Foundation, Leadership Foundation of Greater Las Vegas for the economic development and growth of greater Las Vegas, Nevada.
